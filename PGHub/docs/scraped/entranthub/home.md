## Platform Guide

Not sure where to start? Here's a quick overview of each platform we track.

LeetCode

Interview Prep

Where most people grind for tech interviews. Weekly and biweekly rated contests.

[Browse Events](https://entranthub.com/contests/leetcode)

Codeforces

Competitive

The go-to for competitive programmers. Div 1-4 contests run 2-3 times a week with a massive problem archive.

[Visit Platform](https://codeforces.com/)

AtCoder

Quality Problems

Japanese platform with beautifully crafted problems. ABC contests are beginner-friendly, ARC/AGC are not.

[Visit Platform](https://atcoder.jp/)

CodeChef

Learning Resources

Indian platform running weekly Starters contests (2-3 hours). Good learning resources and an active community.

[Visit Platform](https://www.codechef.com/)

Kaggle

ML & Data

Data science and ML competitions with real datasets. Public notebooks and forums help you learn from the community.

[Browse Events](https://entranthub.com/competitions)

Devpost

Hackathons

Find hackathons to build projects on a deadline. Ship something, win prizes, and pad your portfolio.

[Browse Events](https://entranthub.com/hackathons)

## Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you track?

For coding contests, we only plan to track LeetCode, Codeforces, AtCoder, and CodeChef. Our future focus will be on other tech-related events like hackathons, AI/ML competitions, and tech conferences worldwide. Custom event submissions will also be supported.

How often is the data updated?

We update contest and competition data hourly from all platforms. Conference data is updated daily. You can always check the original source for the most current information.

Is EntrantHub free to use?

Yes! EntrantHub is completely free. Our mission is to help developers discover opportunities and never miss an important event.

How do I get notified about upcoming events?

I'm working on email notifications and calendar integrations. For now, please bookmark this site and check back regularly.
